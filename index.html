Spryker Migration Analyzer

What is the purpose of this tool?
To help you understand what is needed to keep your Spryker project up to date with our Product Releases.
For who is this tool?
Developer, Product Owner/Manager that wants to understand the migration path for their Spryker project.

Product Releases

This section displays the next Spryker Product Releases available to your Spryker project.

Some Product Releases will require databases migrations or some changes to be done on your code or nothing at all.
